About Me

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at the University of Sydney, working under the supervision of Andrew Mathas and Dani Tubbenhauer.

My research interests broadly lie in Representation Theory, Algebraic Combinatorics, and Number Theory. Specifically, I am interested in KLR algebras, crystal bases, and their connections to geometry.
