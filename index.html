About Me

I am a PhD student in Mathematics at the University of Sydney, working under the supervision of Andrew Mathas and Dani Tubbenhauer.

My research interests broadly lie in representation theory, algebraic combinatorics, and related topics.

Specifically, I am interested in KLR (quiver Hecke) algebras, Hecke algebras, and the associated combinatorics.
