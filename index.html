About

I am a PhD candidate in pure mathematics at the University of Sydney . My research lies in representation theory and algebraic combinatorics, with a particular focus on KLR (quiver Hecke) algebras, alcove geometry, and the combinatorics of cores and weights.

我目前在悉尼大学数学与统计学院攻读纯数学博士，主要研究方向为表示论与代数组合学， 包括 KLR 代数、alcove 几何以及 e-芯/e-权等相关组合结构。

Research interests

  • Alcove geometry and Kazhdan–Lusztig theory
  • Algebraic combinatorics of partitions, cores and weights
  • Related topics in number theory
