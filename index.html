About

I am a PhD candidate in pure mathematics at the University of Sydney (Oct 2022 – Feb 2026), supervised by Andrew Mathas and Dani Tubbenhauer. My research interests lie in representation theory and algebraic combinatorics, with a particular focus on KLR (quiver Hecke) algebras, alcove geometry and related combinatorics.

Prior to my PhD, I obtained a Master in Mathematics from HSE University (Moscow), and a Bachelor in Mathematics (Honors College) from Beihang University, including an exchange year at Texas A&M University.

Research interests

  • Representation theory of KLR (quiver Hecke) and KLRW algebras
  • Alcove geometry and affine Weyl groups
  • Decomposition numbers and Specht modules
  • Algebraic combinatorics of partitions, cores and weights
  • Number theory and arithmetic aspects (earlier work)
